Top Universities Around the World

As shown in the map below the top universities are concentrated in the United States and the United Kingdom. The number 1 ranked university in 2023 is the University of Oxford and this is its seventh consecutive year at the top according to Times Higher Education






Here is a scatter plot of the top 200 universities in the world in 2023. It can be seen that there is a positive correlation between a smaller no of students and the overall score of the university with higher ranked universities like Oxford University expemplifying this. Mousing over the open rings will give more information about the university name and their ranking.

The Country selection drop down filter allows for regions with several universities in the top 200 to be viewed in isolation
